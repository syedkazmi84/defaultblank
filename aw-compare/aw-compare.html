Why three paths cluster in the $10,000–$12,500 range: Traditional, Hybrid, and Vanity publishers all extract value through royalty share for the life of the book. Traditional takes ~85% forever in exchange for an advance; Hybrid takes 50% for production services plus a fee; Vanity takes ~60% for production plus a fee. The mechanisms differ — the result is the same. Most of what your book earns over five years goes to someone else. AuthorWings takes 0% royalty share; the $5,795 is a one-time fee, paid once, never recurring.
